AppleShooter - Classic Shooting Game

AppleShooter is a challenging shooting game where players must use a bow and arrow to precisely shoot apples placed on people's heads. The game tests players' accuracy, patience, and skill, with each shot potentially bringing victory or defeat.

Gameplay

Basic Controls

  • Use the mouse to aim: Move the mouse to adjust the direction of the arrow
  • Hold left mouse button: Charge, arrow power increases with hold time
  • Release left mouse button: Fire the arrow

Game Objectives

  • Accurately hit the apple, avoid hitting the person
  • Each level has different difficulties and challenges
  • Get high scores through precise shooting

Game Features

  • Physics engine: Realistic arrow flight trajectory
  • Increasing difficulty: Gradually increasing game difficulty
  • Instant feedback: Intuitive hit effects

Aiming Scene - Precise shooting angle calculation

Successful Hit - Arrow precisely hits the apple

Game History

The inspiration for AppleShooter comes from the Swiss folk legend of William Tell. In the legend, he was forced to shoot an apple placed on his son's head with a bow and arrow, demonstrating amazing archery skills. This story inspired the game development team to transform this thrilling scene into a challenging casual game.

Development History

  • 2005: Game concept born, early development started
  • 2006: First Flash version released
  • 2010: Improved version launched with new game mechanics
  • 2015: HTML5 version released, supporting mobile devices
  • 2020: Major upgrade, optimizing game experience
