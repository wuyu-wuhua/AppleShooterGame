How to Play

1

Start the Game

Click the "Play Now" button to start your AppleShooter adventure. The game will begin with a tutorial level to help you get familiar with the controls.

2

Controls

Use your mouse to aim and click to shoot. The apples will appear at different positions and speeds. Your goal is to hit as many apples as possible.

3

Scoring

Each apple you hit gives you points. The faster you hit the apple, the more points you earn. Special golden apples give bonus points!

4

Power-ups

Collect power-ups to enhance your shooting abilities. These include time slowers, multi-shots, and score multipliers.

5

Levels

Progress through increasingly challenging levels. Each level introduces new obstacles and faster apples to test your skills.

Pro Tips

Aim Ahead

Anticipate the apple's movement and aim slightly ahead of its current position.

Timing is Key

Wait for the perfect moment to shoot for maximum points.

Power-up Strategy

Save power-ups for difficult levels or when you're going for high scores.
