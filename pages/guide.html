Basic Controls Guide

Aiming System

  • Use mouse movement to adjust bow aim direction
  • Aiming line shows predicted arrow trajectory
  • Watch wind indicator, it affects arrow flight path

Shooting Mechanism

  • Hold left mouse button to charge
  • Charge bar shows current power
  • Higher power means faster arrow speed
  • Release left mouse button to shoot

Special Techniques

  • Quick Click: Fast clicks for rapid shots
  • Perfect Charge: Release at optimal position for bonus points
  • Combo System: Consecutive hits earn combo rewards

Level Guide

Beginner Levels (1-5)

  • Stationary targets, close range
  • No wind interference
  • Tip: Master basic controls and aiming

Intermediate Levels (6-10)

  • Slowly moving targets
  • Light wind effects
  • Tip: Learn to predict movement and wind compensation

Advanced Levels (11-15)

  • Fast moving targets
  • Strong wind interference
  • Tip: Master advanced techniques like quick shots

Ultimate Levels (16-20)

  • Irregular target movement
  • Changing wind direction and strength
  • Tip: Combine all techniques for success

Advanced Tips

Wind Compensation

  • Observe wind indicator direction and strength
  • Reduce charge with tailwind, increase with headwind
  • Adjust aim position for crosswinds

Moving Target Prediction

  • Study target movement patterns
  • Calculate arrow flight time
  • Aim ahead of target movement

Perfect Charge Timing

  • Special sound indicates optimal charge position
  • Perfect charge earns bonus points
  • Different distances require different charge times

Scoring System

Basic Scoring

  • Hit apple: 100 points
  • Perfect charge: +50 points
  • Quick hit: +30 points

Combo Rewards

  • 2 hits: 1.2x multiplier
  • 3 hits: 1.5x multiplier
  • 4+ hits: 2x multiplier

Special Rewards

  • Double hit: +200 points
  • Perfect level: +500 points
  • No-miss level: +1000 points

Achievements

Novice Achievements

  • First Hit: Complete first successful shot
  • Basic Mastery: 5 consecutive hits
  • Beginner Archer: Complete first 5 levels

Advanced Achievements

  • Intermediate Archer: Complete first 10 levels
  • Expert Archer: Complete first 15 levels
  • Perfect Archer: Perfect scores on all levels

Master Achievements

  • Archery Master: Complete all levels
  • Perfect Master: No-miss completion of all levels
  • Legendary Archer: Earn all achievements
